# Axiom Maths

**Helping children realise the potential of their power.**

We're a UK education charity on a mission to ensure that access to mathematical excellence doesn't depend on a child's background. Every year, children who were top performers at the end of primary school don't go on to get top GCSE grades in maths. Top-attaining pupils on free school meals are disproportionately affected. We exist to change that.

---

## What we do

We partner with state schools across England and Wales to run **Maths Circles** - small weekly groups where pupils tackle a curriculum of genuinely challenging problems, guided by a trained mentor. Students join in Year 7 and stay with us through to Year 11. The programme is entirely free to schools.

Maths Circles aren't about acceleration or competition. They're about depth, curiosity, and community - a space where pupils who love maths can be themselves, get stuck on hard problems, and find their way through together.

Pupils in our circles:

- Work through problems that take them beyond the National Curriculum
- Learn to articulate their thinking and listen to others
- Build confidence by getting comfortable being stuck - and unstuck
- Find a peer group where mathematical curiosity is valued

We call these **AAA behaviours**: Adventurous, Articulate, and Accepting. They're the foundations of mathematical excellence, and they stay with pupils long after they leave the circle.
